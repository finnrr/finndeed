Reading through job descriptions can be tiring, with this tool you can get a quick estimation on the suitability of a job, tailored to your personal preferences. The longer you use the tool the more it learns about you and the more accurate its recommendations become.

Search for jobs in the ‘new jobs’ section, scrape their contents either one by one (faster) or all at once (slow), click on the job title to see if they’re any good and apply your vote, then transfer the job to your job vault so it can enter the training set. If the job is scraped you can get a prediction based on previous likes/dislikes. Once you have enough new jobs in the job vault hit ‘Retrain’ to generate a new prediction model.

Note: this is an instance for a single user
